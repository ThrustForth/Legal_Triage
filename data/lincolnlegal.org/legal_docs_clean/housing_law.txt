What we do
Housing Law
We help families and individuals who are dealing with housing issues. If you are facing eviction, foreclosure, or another housing issue, know that you are not alone, and help is available to you. We can help with:
Landlord/renter disagreements (including evictions) from private, public or subsidized housing.
Unsafe living conditions.
Utility services.
Rights of applicants and residents of housing subsidized by the government.
Rights of tenants of mobile home parks.
Mortgage foreclosures, contract-for-deed problems and unfair loans.
Assistance in keeping your home.
Fair housing issues, such as discrimination due to race, sex, disability, the presence of children in the family, etc.
Bankruptcy protection to preserve or obtain housing.
